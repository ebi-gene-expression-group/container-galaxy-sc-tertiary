Quickstart

What is this?

An environment where you can do downstreaming analysis for Single Cell transcriptomics. We provide decomposed analysis steps from Seurat, Scanpy, Scater and SC3 to do clustering analysis, starting from 10x formatted data, EMBL-EBI Single Cell Expression Atlas studies or Loom files (Scanpy only currently). You can visualise results using the integrated UCSC CellBrowser. Check out our example workflows.
